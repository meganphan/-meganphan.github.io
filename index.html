About Me

My name is Hoang Phan but I also go by Megan. I come from Da Nang, Vietnam and am currently majoring in Computer Science and Mathematics at Texas Christian University, Fort Worth, TX. I am interested in, but not limited to machine learning and web development.

I am working with my professor on research about Generative Adversarial Networks. My goal is to comprehend the concepts of machine learning and its applications. Additionally, I am currently enrolled in the Machine Learning by Andrew Ng on Coursera.

When not coding, I enjoy playing with my cat, cooking, and playing the ukulele. I am always eager to learn more and excited to contribute my skillsets to our community.
